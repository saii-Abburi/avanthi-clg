ABOUT AVANTHI

Avanthi's Scientific Technological & Research Academy Hyderabad is sponsored by Avanthi Educational Society, established in the year [text unclear], with the able leadership of Sri. M. Srinivasa Rao. It came into being with a congregation of dedicated academicians and able administrators in the field of higher education and learning. The society has grown into a large group of more than 13 educational institutions, having a student strength of more than 16,000, pursuing education in the fields of Engineering & Technology, Science, Commerce, IT, and Management under this fold.
